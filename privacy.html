Privacy Policy — Pohoda Webparty Monitor

Last updated: June 2026

Pohoda Webparty Monitor is a browser extension that monitors the public Webparty forum at pohodafestival.sk/webparty for new posts and alerts you according to your settings.

Data we collect

The extension does not collect, sell, or transmit your personal data to the developer or any third party.

  • Local settings — blocked usernames, keywords, check interval, notification preferences, and language are stored only in your browser using Chrome’s local storage (chrome.storage.local).
  • Public forum content — the extension reads publicly visible Webparty posts from pohodafestival.sk to detect new posts and show previews in notifications and history. This content is processed locally on your device.

What we do not do

  • No accounts or login
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No data sold to third parties
  • No remote code execution — all extension code is bundled in the package

Permissions

The extension requests browser permissions only to perform its stated function: checking the Webparty page, showing notifications, playing an optional alert sound, and saving your preferences locally.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer via the email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing for this extension.
